Our Story

Moon Founder Justin Keoninh's parents fled their war-torn homeland of Laos in the 1980s and spent two years in a Thai refugee camp before deciding where they would start their new life. When Justin asked his father why he chose to come to the United States, Le told him, “I wanted to come to America because they put men on the moon.”

The moon landing, one of America's greatest feats, showed us the degree at which technology can influence humanity. It serves as the main inspiration for Moon and represents how we want to devote our skills to the shifts happening in the world.

Our People
